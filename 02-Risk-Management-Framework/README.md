# Project 02 — Risk Management Framework

## Overview
A complete Risk Management Framework for FinSecure Pvt Ltd 
covering risk identification, scoring, heat mapping, 
risk appetite, and treatment plans.

## Files
- FinSecure-Risk-Register-v1.0.xlsx
- FinSecure-Risk-Appetite-Statement-v1.0.docx

## Risk Areas Covered
- Data Privacy & PII Breach
- Cyber & Information Security  
- Regulatory & Compliance
- Third-Party & Vendor Risk
- Fraud & Financial Crime

## Frameworks Referenced
ISO 27001 · DPDP Act 2023 · RBI NBFC Master Directions · SOC 2
