# Project 05 — GRC Metrics & Board Reporting

## Overview
A complete GRC Board Reporting pack for FinSecure Pvt Ltd
summarising risk posture, compliance coverage, audit readiness,
and key risk indicators across all 5 risk categories.
Designed for presentation to the Board Risk Committee.

## Files
- `FinSecure-GRC-Board-Report-v1.0.xlsx`

## Tabs Inside the Excel
- **Risk Posture Dashboard** — 13 KPI cards · risk by category · controls status · executive commentary
- **Compliance Coverage** — 8 frameworks scored · gap analysis · action owners
- **Audit Readiness** — RBI audit readiness score 68% · 7 categories · action plan to reach 85%
- **KRI Scorecard** — 16 Key Risk Indicators · RAG status · trend arrows · summary cards

## Key Finding
AWS concentration risk is the single Red KRI — 100% cloud
dependency on one vendor against a limit of 80%. Multi-cloud
contingency plan required by Q4 2026.
