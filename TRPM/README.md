# Project 03 — Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM)

## Overview
A complete Third-Party Risk Management program for FinSecure Pvt Ltd 
covering vendor identification, risk tiering, due diligence, ongoing 
monitoring, and vendor lifecycle management.

## Files
- FinSecure-Vendor-Register-v1.0.xlsx
- FinSecure-Vendor-DDQ-v1.0.docx
- FinSecure-TPRM-Policy-v1.0.docx

## Vendors Covered
- Amazon Web Services (AWS) — Cloud Infrastructure — Critical
- TransUnion CIBIL — Credit Bureau — Critical
- Razorpay — Payment Gateway — Critical
- Experian India — Credit Bureau (Secondary) — High
- DigiLocker (MeitY) — eKYC Verification — Medium
- Perfios — Bank Statement Analysis — High
- Twilio — SMS OTP Gateway — Medium
- AuthBridge — Background Verification — Medium

## Risk Scoring Dimensions
- Data Sensitivity
- Business Criticality
- Security Posture
- Regulatory Compliance
- Financial Stability

## Frameworks Referenced
RBI Outsourcing Guidelines 2023 · ISO 27001 A.5.19–22 · DPDP Act 2023 · PMLA 2002
